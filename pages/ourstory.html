December 2022
When they met
Daniel and Lina went on their first date at Ambar India near UC - where they shared their love of Indian food (Chicken Tikka Masala for Lina and Lamb Mushroom for Daniel)
January 2023
When they came together
In a scene straight out of a romantic movie, Lina and Daniel crossed paths at the bustling Amtrak terminal in DC during his brief 20-minute stopover. With the backdrop of hurried commuters and echoing announcements, Daniel seized the moment and popped the question, asking Lina to be his girlfriend.
Spring 2023
When they had fun
Lina and Daniel set a simple yet sweet goal: a Friday night date every week. They mixed things up with dinners aboard the BB Riverboat, bowling at Pin's, and winding down with drinks at Ghost Baby.
Fall 2023
When they lived apart
With Lina starting her new job near NYC and Daniel staying in Cincinnati, the two would ping-pong between the two cities.
March 10, 2024
When they promised to be together forever
During a trip to Japan to visit Lina's grandparents, Daniel found the perfect moment to propose to her outside the serene Arashiyama Bamboo Forest.
